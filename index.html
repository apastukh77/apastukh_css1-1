History

Before the war, endless reforms were carried out in the Armed Forces of Ukraine, most often the reforms were with optimization of the number of military units and subunits. 2013 was marked by numerical reductions for the Armed Forces of Ukraine. As a result of the next criminal reform in 2013 by the decree of the President of Ukraine instead of 3 bodies of territorial management of Land forces 2, on the basis of 6 AK and 13 AK were created:

  1. Operational Command “South” (Dnipropetrovsk)
  2. Operational Command “North” (Rivne)

The commander of the 6th Army Corps general Ruslan Khomchak became the commander of the operational command "South". Under the command of the Operational Command "South" were military units and subunits of the Land Forces, as well as local military authorities, which were territorially located in 13 regions of Ukraine.

Operational Command "South" lasted until 2015.

Operational Command “South” HQ, A1314 was responsible for the formation and operational management of units and subunits in Sector B.

With the beginning of the Russian aggression, from the first days of combat actions in the eastern Ukraine, the officers of the A1314 military unit were on the front line. At that time, staff officers were involved in military units that went to the combat zone to increase the combat capability of units on the spot, took part in planning and conducting operational and combat support of units and subunits in Sector B , provided organizational assistance to unit commanders on the front line, took a direct part in the battles.

One of the most tragic pages of the military unit A1314 is connected with Ilovaisk Tragedy .


The Sector B is the is a group of Ukrainian military units west of the city of Donetsk during the war in eastern Ukraine in 2014-2015 by commanding of general Khomchak.
